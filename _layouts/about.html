About Philanware

Empowering nonprofits with purpose-built software that amplifies impact, improves efficiency, and strengthens donor relationships.

Our Story

Founded in 2019, Philanware was created to bridge the technological gap in the nonprofit sector. We noticed that many mission-driven organizations were burdened by outdated tools and manual workflows that slowed down their progress.

Our team of developers, nonprofit strategists, and product experts set out to build software tailored specifically to the needs of nonprofits—from grant management and donor tracking to volunteer coordination and reporting. We've now partnered with over 150 organizations across the globe, delivering intuitive tools that allow them to focus more on their mission and less on their admin.
